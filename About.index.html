About Us

Fairytale Day Care aims to provide quality childcare and learning experiences for children, in a safe and caring environment. Through the development of social, creative, physical and intellectual learning skills, we thrive to enrich their development by implementing vast educational curriculums customized specifically to each child’s needs.

Our highly trained staff have vast experience and come from an array of prestigious universities. We have developed a unique approach to further develope your child's ability to grow.

The combination of this approach will create a bond between the teacher and your child. This will thoroughly prepare him/her for their next educational endeavor. Our state of the art facility and our latest learning equipment (i.e, Smart Boards, tablets) will create a one of a kind experience, that will utilize and challenge kids on every level: from motor skills, language arts, sciences, socio-emotional, numeracy, creative arts, music, foreign languages to the advanced learning programs, as well as extracurricular activities ranging from music, vocal lessons, Zumba-Jr, dance classes to chess and much more
